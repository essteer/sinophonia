Hsieh Tzu-fan 謝子凡

Hsieh Tzu-fan 謝子凡 is an author, translator, and former advertiser.

Me and the Garbage Truck I Chased 《我和我追逐的垃圾車》 is a collection of semi-autobiographical short stories. Divided across four themes (Hot - Cold - Dark - Light), Xie's restrained but reflective writing style gives depth to her everyday subject matter.
