Xue Yiwei 薛憶潙

Xue Yiwei 薛憶溈 (1964 - ) is an author, academic, and doctor of linguistics.

His first novel, Desertion 《遺棄》, uses a first-person narrative to explore the protagonist's philosophical reflections, as he struggles against bureaucratic opposition to quit his vacuous government job.

With Shenzheners 《深圳人》, Xue took inspiration from James Joyce's Dubliners to produce these short stories on fictional residents of the Chinese city.
