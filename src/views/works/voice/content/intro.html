VOICE 嗓

Presented here are audio materials in the form of interviews, lectures and podcasts across a wide range of subjects.

Two series of particular note are Gushi FM故事FM, whose producers coax out the personal stories and experiences of ordinary people in monologue form, and the Bu Mingbai不明白 podcast, in which the host explores her guests' perspectives of ongoing social and political developments.

See the SOURCES page for suggestions on where to find Chinese podcast apps and series.

These provide valuable insight into discussions taking place in sinophone communities, and a sense of the breadth of opinions and incentives at play beyond the narratives typical of mainstream outlets.

In a different vein, The Memory Project is a large-scale oral history project consisting of interviews with people who lived through significant periods of social disruption during the 20th century.
