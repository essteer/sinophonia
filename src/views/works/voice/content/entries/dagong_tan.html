Dagong Tan 打工談

Over 46 episodes released between 2020 and 2023, the hosts of Dagong Tan打工談 — 'Labour Talks' — spend time with manual labourers and people from other working-class professions to discuss how they make their living, the social status of rural and blue-collar workers, and the struggles they face in their work and personal lives.

The hosts show genuine empathy towards their guests, who are given room to voice their experiences with a dignity that contrasts with the prevailing social mood towards the so-called 'low-end population' 低端人口 responsible for the PRC's prosperity.

Elsewhere the discourse on this major social segment typically reflects official narratives, or takes place in narrow economic terms. Dagong Tan engages with the lived reality of labour as endured by the workers themselves, offering sober accounts of the often exploitative nature of work in the People's Republic.
