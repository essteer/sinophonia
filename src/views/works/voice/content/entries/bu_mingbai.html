Bu Mingbai 不明白

Begun in mid-2022, Bu Mingbai 不明白 is the personal project of a group of professional journalists, hosted by New York Times reporter Yuan Li 袁莉.

In the words of its creators, the Bu Mingbai podcast "hopes to share interesting conversations with a global Chinese-language audience, to shine a little light and warmth during this dark and chaotic era."

Yuan Li interviews guests from a range of professional and social backgrounds to discuss their views on current affairs involving the PRC. Episodes last around an hour, and at the end of each conversation guests are invited to share book recommendations, making it a good source for reading suggestions.

One entry in a slightly different format is episode 12, Twists and Turns in the Chinese Nation's World Entrance 傳統中國人認識世界的曲折和挫折 in which Fudan University 復旦大學 historian Ge Zhaoguang 葛兆光 delivers a seminar on the title subject.

Notable recurring guests of the series include political scientists Cai Xia 蔡霞 and Minxin Pei 裴敏欣, and Stanford research scholars Xu Chenggang 許成鋼 and Wu Guoguang 吳國光. They are joined by a host of other prominent figures as well as members of the general public, who are sometimes anonymised to protect their identities when discussions touch on sensitive topics.
