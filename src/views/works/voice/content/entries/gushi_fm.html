Gushi FM 故事FM

Created by journalist Kou Aizhe 寇愛哲 in 2017, Gushi FM 故事FM is a podcast series that relays personal narratives from ordinary people in their own words. With its 700th episode released in early 2023, it is an incredible resource that provides a platform for voices across the spectrum of human experience.

Recorded as interviews, the podcast team's questions are largely edited out to leave an uninterrupted monologue told by each guest. Most episodes run from 20 to 40 minutes.
