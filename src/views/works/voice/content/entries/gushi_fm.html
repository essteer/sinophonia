Gushi FM 故事FM

Created by journalist Kou Aizhe 寇愛哲 in 2017, Gushi FM 故事FM is a podcast series that relays personal narratives from ordinary people in their own words.

With its 800th episode released in mid-2024, it is an incredible resource that provides a platform for voices across the spectrum of human experience. The series has garnered critical acclaim and considerable popularity for the quality of its production and the consistently engaging oral histories in its extensive backlog.

Following an initial introduction to the guest speaker and the story they will share, the interviewers' questions are typically edited out of each recording to give a sense of an uninterrupted monologue by each guest. Most episodes run from 20 to 40 minutes, with occasional longer stories split across several recordings.
