Eye on East Asia 東亞觀察局

Eye on East Asia東亞觀察局 is a podcast about the politics, culture and history of China, Japan and Korea, and the relationships between them.

The hosts are journalists Fan Yiru 梵一如, Shao Qingqing 少青青 and Quan Xiaoxing 權小星, who together discuss their perspectives of developments involving these countries, and provide political commentary on the nuances of their interactions with and stances towards each other.

For those interested in the politics and culture of East Asia, this series is a useful resource for sustained coverage and analysis, as well as insight into conversations taking place within the PRC about the often tense diplomatic relationships of the region.
