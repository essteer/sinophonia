Wang Shuo 王朔

Wang Shuo 王朔 (1958 - ) is a novelist and screenwriter. Known for his liu mang 流氓 or 'hooligan' style of writing, his works have been popular source material for film and television since the late 1980s.

The dark humour of Please Don't Call Me Human 《千萬別把我當人》 gets close to the bone with its ridicule of totalitarian bureaucracy. Published in the 1980s, a similar work could not evade censorship today.
