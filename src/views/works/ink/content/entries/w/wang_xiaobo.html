Wang Xiaobo 王小波

Wang Xiaobo 王小波 (1952 — 1997) was a novelist and essayist. Born in Beijing, he was amongst the many 'sent-down' or 'educated' youths 知識青年 extracted from urban life to labour in rural areas during the Cultural Revolution 文革.

That experience influenced some of his best-known works, including the satirical Golden Age 《黃金時代》. Essay collections such as Silent Majority 《沉默的大多數》 exhibit his vernacular wit and talent for querying social norms.
