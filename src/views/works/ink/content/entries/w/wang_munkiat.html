Wang Mun Kiat 黄文杰

Wang Mun Kiat 黃文傑 (1967 — ) is a Singaporean poet and engineer.

The poems of his second collection Short Tongue 《短舌》 are haiku-like in their brevity, but Wang manages to imbue their clipped lines with barbed social commentary.

I've spent little time with Chinese poetry in the past, often finding the form's abstract nature to be beyond my language ability.

Some entries of Short Tongue are cryptic, but in general it's accessible and I'd recomend it to others interested in trying poetry in Chinese.

The publisher Sea Breeze Books 海風書屋 also offers a bilingual edition, with English translations by the poet Daryl Lim Wei Jie 林偉傑.
