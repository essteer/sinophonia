Hao Jingfang 郝景芳

Hao Jingfang 郝景芳1984 is a science fiction writer and the 2016 recipient of the Hugo Award for Best Novelette.

Her novella Folding Beijing北京摺曡 is a futuristic riff on the experiences of the eponymous city's beipiao 北漂 or migrant workers, whose labour is essential to its function, and yet must eke out precarious lives in a legal grey zone without basic rights.

The urban future portrayed here is no less dystopian than that of Blade Runner, but feels darker for how conceivable it is. Technological breakthrough hasn't led to replicants and galactic conflict, only to exacerbated wealth inequality and grim tedium for the working poor.
