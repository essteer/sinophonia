Hao Jingfang 郝景芳

Hao Jingfang 郝景芳 (1984 — ) is a science fiction writer and 2016 recipient of the Hugo Award for Best Novelette.

Her novella Folding Beijing 《北京摺曡》 is a futuristic riff on the experiences of the city's beipiao 北漂 or migrant workers, whose labour is essential to its function, and yet must eke out precarious lives in a legal grey zone without basic rights.
