Yu Hua 余華

Yu Hua 余華 (1960 - ) is an author, and committee member of the Chinese Writers Association.

To Live 《活著》 follows the hubristic descent into poverty of the arrogant son of a land-owning family. As the events of civil war, famine, and the Cultural Revolution unfold, the protagonist emerges with a kinder outlook even under increasing isolation.

As a study of an individual, To Live makes a good compliment to Chen Zhongshi's 陳忠實 broader take on similar themes from that era in White Deer Plain 《白鹿原》.
