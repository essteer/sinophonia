Qi Jun 琦君

Qi Jun 琦君 (1917 — 2006) was a writer, essayist, critic and translator.

Her novel Ripe Tangerine 《橘子紅了》 is a critique of female exploitation under traditional family structures. Xiufen 秀芬 becomes the third wife in a rural household, arranged as Dama's 大媽 futile attempt to coax her husband back from the city he has abandoned her for to be with his second wife.

Zhang Yimou 張藝謀 explored similar themes in his film Raise the Red Lantern 《大紅燈籠高高挂》, released the same year in 1991.
