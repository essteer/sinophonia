Feng Tang 馮唐

Feng Tang 馮唐 is the pseudonym of Zhang Haipeng 張海鵬1971, a writer, poet and private equity investor from Beijing.

Oneness不二 depicts Tang dynasty monks and nuns as sexual deviants with cynical attitudes towards their faith and peers. Feng's explicit deadpan humour is reminiscent of the writing of Wang Xiaobo 王小波1952-1997, and he combines this with historical detail and satirical plays on monastic politics to great effect.

The lewd pretext is good fun, but would wear thin if it wasn't matched with such strong execution. Feng clearly knows this and keeps the smut at the level of wit and allusion without lapsing into pornography. I came away wanting to read more about the real history of the era, which is solid praise for a book that is marketed in Hong Kong bookstores as if it were a top-shelf magazine at a newsstand.

But of course that's part of the fun.
