Feng Tang 馮唐

Feng Tang 馮唐 is the pseudonym of Zhang Haipeng 張海鵬 (1971 — ), a writer, poet, and private equity investor from Beijing.

Oneness 《不二》 depicts Tang dynasty monks and nuns as sexual deviants with cynical attitudes towards their faith and peers. Feng's explicit deadpan humour is reminiscent of Wang Xiaobo 王小波, and he combines this with historical detail and satirical plays on monastic politics to great effect.
