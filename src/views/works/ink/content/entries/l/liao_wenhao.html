Liao Wen-hao 廖文豪

Liao Wen-hao 廖文豪1960 is an assistant professor and library director of the National Taipei University of Business 國立臺北商業大學.

Liao is the author of Hanzi Tree 漢字樹, a series of meticulously-researched books on the original meanings behind Chinese characters. Each volume uses vivid graphics to illustrate the genesis of hundreds of characters according to themes such as people or plants.

In countless examples, Liao demonstrates how basic pictographs 象形字 are composed into ideographs that convey complex abstract concepts essential to any advanced society. He also addresses misconceptions regarding characters that have become corrupted over time — not least as a result of simplification — to present their true form.

Aside from the surface-level aspect of gaining deeper appreciation for the beauty of Chinese characters, the aggregate effect of Liao's work is to remind us of how intelligent this writing system is, as well as offer a sense of shared humanity via glimpses into the minds of its designers.
