China Unofficial Archives 中國民間檔案館

The China Unofficial Archives 中國民間檔案館 is a non-profit organisation that launched in December 2023 as a platform "dedicated to making accessible the key documents, movies, blogs, and publications of a movement of Chinese people seeking to reclaim their country's history."

The founder of the archive is Ian Johnson, a Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist and author renowned for his extensive research on grassroots movements in the People's Republic. The organisation is supported by numerous researchers, historians, and anonymous staff members and contributors.

Included among the archives is an extensive back-catalogue of the influential history magazine China Through the Ages炎黄春秋— which enjoyed a reputation as a rare space for modest critiques of the government until its abrupt termination in 2014 — in addition to a growing body of other important writing and films that shed light on the broad scope of discussion and research that persists outside of official narratives.
