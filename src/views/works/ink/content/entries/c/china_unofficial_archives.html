China Unoffical Archives
中國民間檔案館

The China Unoffical Archives launched in December 2023 as a platform "dedicated to making accessible the key documents, movies, blogs, and publications of a movement of Chinese people seeking to reclaim their country's history."

The archives include an extensive back-catalogue of the renowned history magazine China Through the Ages 《炎黄春秋》 — abruptly dismantled in 2014 — in addition to a growing body of other important writing and films that shed light on the broad scope of discussion and research that persists outside of official narratives.
