Creative Comic Collection 創作業

Creative Comic Collection 創作業 is a Taiwanese publication that promotes local comic book artists. CCC was established as a quarterly in 2009 as part of a broader initiative to foster creative works about Taiwan's culture and history.

After a brief hiatus beginning in 2015, popular demand brought CCC back as a monthly publication in 2017 and in 2020 it moved online to continue in digital format.

Comics in CCC exhibit a broad range of visual styles by the many different featured artists. Subject matter is equally varied in tone, from issues such as women's low social status in pre-modern Taiwan or the experience of indigenous people during colonisation, to contemporary themes, horror and sci-fi.
