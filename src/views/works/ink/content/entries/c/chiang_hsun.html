Chiang Hsun 蔣勛

Chiang Hsun 蔣勳 (1947 — ) is a writer, artist, and former professor of fine arts at Taiwan's Tunghai University 東海大學. He has written extensively on global culture, art history, and Chinese history, and is a popular speaker due to his accessible style.

His extensive output includes interpretations of literary classics, such as his mesmerising in-depth series on Dream of the Red Chamber 紅樓夢 — akin to the late Frank Delaney's contribution to James Joyce's Ulysses — essays on Buddhism, and musings on artistic expression across differing cultural and societal values.
