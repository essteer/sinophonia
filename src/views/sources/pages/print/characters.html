Character Variants 繁體與簡體

Depending on how and where you learnt to read Chinese, you may be familiar with either traditional 繁體字 or simplified characters 簡體字.

The latter are officially used in the People's Republic of China and Singapore; traditional characters are still going strong in Hong Kong, Macau, Malaysia, Taiwan, and across much of the global diaspora.

I recommend learning traditional characters to get the most out of the written language in its intended form.

If you began with simplified characters and want to learn traditional — or vice versa — flashcard apps that use spaced repetition can get you up to speed with the other form in a short period of time.
